Key Features of The Maze

Challenging Mazes

Navigate through a series of increasingly difficult mazes that test your logic and problem-solving skills. Each level brings new traps and puzzles to solve.

Unique Traps & Puzzles

Watch out for hidden traps and mind-bending puzzles. Every maze contains a set of obstacles that requires quick thinking and strategy to overcome.

Retro Pixel Art Style

Immerse yourself in a retro-inspired world with beautifully designed pixel art graphics, blending nostalgic charm with modern gameplay mechanics.

About The Maze

The Maze was inspired by my love for classic puzzle games and the challenge of creating something that blends nostalgia with modern gameplay. As a fan of old-school arcade games, I wanted to design a game that captures the essence of those early adventures while adding a new twist.

The journey started in early 2024 when I began working on the game's concept and design. Over the months, I developed the core mechanics and pixel art style, drawing from my experiences and the inspiration from iconic maze games. This project has been a rewarding challenge, allowing me to apply my skills in game development and design.

This project is part of my portfolio for ALX Software Engineering programme, where I have gained valuable skills in software development. You can find the complete source code and additional details on the GitHub repository.
